Classic Green Tea

10 sachets

Varietal
JinXuan
Harvest
April
Oxidation
3-5%
Certification
Tse-Xin Organic
Temperature
194°F / 90°C
Method
Place one sachet in a cup, pour hot water, and steep for 3-5 minutes. Can be re-steeped multiple times.

Mung Bean · Cane Sugar

A light, refreshing green tea with natural sweetness. The JinXuan varietal imparts a gentle mung bean note with a clean, sugarcane finish.
